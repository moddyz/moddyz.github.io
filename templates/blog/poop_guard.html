Poop Guard Part 1: The idea

Dec 3, 2025

My cat Puff has pooping on the floor since she was a kitten.

She has not one, but two littertrays. But NOPE, she prefers to number 2 right on the floor.

I've been wanting to try a electronics project for a while. After cleaning the poop off the today I was inspired - why don't I build a robot encourage her not to poop on the floor?

The thing is, I'm certain she knows it's the wrong thing to do. Because there was a couple times I'd be watching TV. I'd see her approach the poop corner and begin to squat. If I make a gesture or sound - she'll meow, and go do her business in the littertray.
